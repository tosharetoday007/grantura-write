Set up GKS Write

Follow these steps and you'll be writing in minutes

1

Get a free AI API key

GKS Write uses your own AI key — you connect directly to the AI provider, your content never passes through our servers. All three options below offer a free tier to get started.

🤖 Anthropic Claude ✨ Google Gemini ⚡ Groq (fastest)
2

Open GKS Write sidebar

Click the GKS Write icon in your Firefox toolbar to open the sidebar. Then go to the Settings tab (⚙️), paste your API key, choose your AI provider, and click Save.

3

Save your first reference

Go to any paper on PubMed, Europe PMC, or any journal website. Click the GKS Write toolbar button — your reference will be captured automatically with all fields filled in. Click Save.

4

Start writing your manuscript

Open the sidebar and go to the Write tab. Choose a section (Introduction, Methods, Results, etc.), describe what you want, and click Generate. GKS Write will write the section and automatically insert citations from your library.
